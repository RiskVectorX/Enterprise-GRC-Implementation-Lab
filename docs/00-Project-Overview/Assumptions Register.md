# Executive Summary

This Assumptions Register documents the key business, technical, regulatory, and project assumptions that form the basis of the Enterprise GRC Implementation Lab.

These assumptions ensure consistency across all project deliverables and provide context for governance decisions, risk assessments, security control selection, and compliance activities.

Should any assumption change during the engagement, the associated deliverables must be reviewed and updated accordingly.

---

# Purpose

The objectives of this register are to:

- Establish a common understanding of project assumptions.
- Reduce ambiguity during decision-making.
- Provide traceability for future updates.
- Support consistent risk assessment throughout the engagement.

# Assumption Review Process

Project assumptions will be reviewed:

- At the completion of each project phase.
- Following significant business or technology changes.
- When new regulatory requirements are introduced.
- During management review meetings.

Any assumption determined to be invalid will be documented, evaluated for impact, and reflected in relevant project deliverables.
