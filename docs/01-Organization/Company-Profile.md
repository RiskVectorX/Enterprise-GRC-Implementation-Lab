# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this document is to provide an overview of FinSure Technologies Pvt. Ltd., including its business operations, organizational profile, products, services, technology environment, and key business characteristics.

This document establishes the organizational context used throughout the Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) program and serves as the foundation for subsequent governance, risk management, compliance, and information security documentation.

---

# 2. Company Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Company Name | FinSure Technologies Pvt. Ltd. |
| Industry | Financial Technology (FinTech) |
| Headquarters | Bengaluru, Karnataka, India |
| Founded | 2020 |
| Employees | Approximately 240 |
| Business Model | Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) |
| Primary Market | India |
| Business Type | Private Limited Company |

FinSure Technologies develops cloud-native financial software solutions that enable small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) to digitize financial operations, automate payment processing, and manage regulatory compliance through secure online platforms.

The organization delivers subscription-based software services hosted on Microsoft Azure and serves customers across multiple regulated industries.

---

# 3. Business Overview

FinSure Technologies specializes in providing modern cloud-based financial services designed for businesses that require secure, scalable, and compliant digital financial operations.

Its solutions are built using modern cloud technologies and follow a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) delivery model, allowing customers to securely access services over the internet without maintaining on-premises infrastructure.

The company places significant emphasis on:

- Information Security
- Data Privacy
- Regulatory Compliance
- Service Availability
- Customer Trust
- Continuous Innovation
