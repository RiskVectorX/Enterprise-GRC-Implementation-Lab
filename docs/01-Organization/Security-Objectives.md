# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this document is to define the strategic information security objectives of FinSure Technologies Pvt. Ltd. These objectives support the organization's business goals by ensuring that information assets, technology services, and business operations remain secure, resilient, and compliant.

The objectives provide direction for the Information Security Management System (ISMS) and establish measurable outcomes for governance, risk management, and continual improvement activities.

---

# 2. Scope

These objectives apply to all employees, contractors, third-party service providers, information systems, business processes, and information assets that fall within the scope of the organization's ISMS.

---

# 3. Information Security Objectives

The organization has established the following strategic information security objectives.

## Objective 1 – Protect Information Assets

Safeguard organizational and customer information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Key Focus Areas:

- Information classification
- Access control
- Encryption
- Secure storage
- Data lifecycle management

---

## Objective 2 – Maintain Service Availability

Ensure that critical business services remain available and resilient to support customer operations and business continuity.

Key Focus Areas:

- High availability
- Backup and recovery
- Disaster recovery
- Capacity management
- Infrastructure resilience

---

## Objective 3 – Strengthen Cybersecurity Posture

Continuously improve the organization's ability to prevent, detect, respond to, and recover from cybersecurity threats.

Key Focus Areas:

- Security monitoring
- Vulnerability management
- Threat detection
- Incident response
- Continuous monitoring

---

## Objective 4 – Manage Enterprise Risk

Identify, assess, treat, and monitor information security risks using a structured risk management process aligned with organizational risk appetite.

Key Focus Areas:

- Risk assessments
- Risk register
- Risk treatment
- Residual risk monitoring
- Executive risk reporting

---

## Objective 5 – Ensure Regulatory Compliance

Maintain compliance with applicable legal, regulatory, contractual, and industry requirements.

Key Focus Areas:

- ISO/IEC 27001
- PCI DSS
- GDPR
- DPDP Act
- Internal policies

---

## Objective 6 – Secure Third-Party Relationships

Manage security risks arising from vendors, suppliers, cloud providers, and external service providers.

Key Focus Areas:

- Vendor due diligence
- Security assessments
- Contractual requirements
- Continuous monitoring
- Risk acceptance

---

## Objective 7 – Promote Security Awareness

Develop a security-conscious culture by providing employees with ongoing education, awareness, and role-specific training.

Key Focus Areas:

- Security awareness
- Phishing simulations
- Policy acknowledgement
- Secure development practices

---

# 4. Success Measures

Achievement of these objectives will be evaluated through measurable indicators including:

- Reduction in high-risk findings.
- Timely completion of risk assessments.
- Improved audit results.
- Increased policy compliance.
- Reduced security incidents.
- High availability of critical services.
- Timely remediation of vulnerabilities.
- Completion of mandatory security awareness training.

---

# 5. Governance and Review

Security objectives shall be:

- Reviewed at least annually.
- Evaluated during Management Review meetings.
- Updated following significant business or regulatory changes.
- Monitored using defined Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and Key Risk Indicators (KRIs).

---

# 6. Relationship to the GRC Program

These objectives provide strategic direction for:

- Information Security Management System (ISMS)
- Enterprise Risk Management
- Security Governance
- Policy Development
- Security Controls
- Third-Party Risk Management
- Business Continuity
- Compliance Management
- Internal Audit
- Executive Reporting
