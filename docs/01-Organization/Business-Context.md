# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this document is to establish the internal and external business context within which FinSure Technologies Pvt. Ltd. operates. Understanding the business context ensures that governance, risk management, compliance, and information security activities align with organizational objectives, stakeholder expectations, regulatory obligations, and operational priorities.

---

# 2. Organizational Context

FinSure Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a cloud-native Financial Technology (FinTech) Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) provider that develops secure digital financial solutions for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs).

The organization delivers business-critical financial services through Microsoft Azure, enabling customers to manage digital payments, financial operations, merchant services, and regulatory reporting through a secure online platform.

As a technology-driven organization, FinSure relies heavily on cloud infrastructure, third-party service providers, and continuous software delivery to support business growth and customer satisfaction.

---

# 3. Mission

To simplify and secure digital financial operations by delivering reliable, innovative, and compliant cloud-based financial technology solutions.

---

# 4. Vision

To become a trusted provider of secure and compliant financial technology platforms for businesses across India while maintaining high standards of information security, privacy, and operational resilience.

---

# 5. Strategic Business Objectives

The organization has identified the following strategic objectives:

- Deliver secure and highly available SaaS services.
- Protect customer information and business-critical assets.
- Maintain compliance with applicable legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.
- Strengthen customer trust through effective security governance.
- Improve operational efficiency through automation and cloud-native technologies.
- Support sustainable business growth while effectively managing enterprise risk.

---

# 6. Internal Context

Key internal factors influencing the organization's GRC program include:

## Business Model

- Subscription-based SaaS platform.
- Cloud-first operating model.
- Continuous software development and deployment.
- Rapid feature delivery through DevSecOps practices.

## Technology Environment

- Microsoft Azure cloud infrastructure.
- Containerized applications hosted on Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS).
- Centralized identity management through Microsoft Entra ID.
- Security monitoring using Microsoft Sentinel.
- CI/CD pipelines managed through GitHub Actions.

## Organizational Characteristics

- Approximately 240 employees.
- Cross-functional teams including Engineering, Security, Cloud Operations, Product Management, Customer Success, Legal & Compliance, and Finance.
- Hybrid work environment with secure remote access capabilities.

# 7. External Context

The organization operates within an environment influenced by several external factors.

## Regulatory Environment

Business operations are subject to multiple legal and regulatory obligations relating to information security, privacy, financial data protection, and payment processing.

## Customers

Customers expect:

- High platform availability.
- Secure handling of financial information.
- Strong privacy protections.
- Regulatory compliance.
- Reliable customer support.

## Third-Party Dependencies

The organization depends on external providers for:

- Cloud infrastructure.
- Productivity services.
- Software development tools.
- Security monitoring technologies.
- Payment processing integrations.

## Cyber Threat Landscape

The organization faces evolving cyber threats including:

- Phishing attacks.
- Credential theft.
- Ransomware.
- Cloud misconfigurations.
- Insider threats.
- Third-party compromise.
- Supply chain attacks.
