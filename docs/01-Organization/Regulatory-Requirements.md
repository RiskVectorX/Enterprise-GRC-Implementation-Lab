# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this document is to identify the legal, regulatory, contractual, and industry requirements applicable to FinSure Technologies Pvt. Ltd.

Understanding these obligations enables the organization to establish appropriate governance, implement effective security controls, manage compliance risks, and demonstrate due diligence to customers, regulators, and business partners.

---

# 2. Scope

This document applies to all business units, employees, contractors, information systems, information assets, third-party service providers, and business processes that support the organization's cloud-based financial technology services.

---

# 3. Regulatory Landscape

As a cloud-native FinTech organization, FinSure Technologies operates within a regulatory environment requiring the protection of financial information, personal data, and business-critical systems.

The organization maintains a Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) program aligned with recognized international standards and applicable legal requirements.

---

# 4. Applicable Standards and Regulations

## ISO/IEC 27001:2022

Purpose:

Establishes the requirements for implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an Information Security Management System (ISMS).

Business Relevance:

- Information security governance
- Risk management
- Security controls
- Continual improvement

---

## ISO/IEC 27002:2022

Purpose:

Provides implementation guidance for information security controls supporting ISO/IEC 27001.

Business Relevance:

- Control implementation
- Security best practices
- Operational security

---

## NIST Risk Management Framework (RMF)

Purpose:

Provides a structured approach for managing information security risk throughout the system lifecycle.

Business Relevance:

- Risk governance
- Risk assessment
- Security authorization
- Continuous monitoring

---

## NIST SP 800-30 Rev.1

Purpose:

Defines the organization's methodology for conducting information security risk assessments.

Business Relevance:

- Threat identification
- Vulnerability assessment
- Risk analysis
- Risk evaluation

---

## NIST SP 800-53

Purpose:

Provides a catalog of security and privacy controls.

Business Relevance:

- Security control selection
- Control implementation
- Control assessment

---

## PCI DSS

Purpose:

Protects payment card information through security and operational controls.

Business Relevance:

- Payment processing
- Secure system configuration
- Access management
- Monitoring

---

## General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

Purpose:

Protects the personal data and privacy rights of individuals within the European Union.

Business Relevance:

- Privacy governance
- Data subject rights
- Cross-border data protection
- Privacy risk management

---

## Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, India

Purpose:

Establishes requirements for processing digital personal data within India.

Business Relevance:

- Personal data protection
- Consent management
- Data processing obligations
- Individual privacy rights

---

# 5. Contractual Requirements

In addition to regulatory obligations, the organization must comply with contractual security requirements established through agreements with:

- Customers
- Cloud service providers
- Technology vendors
- Business partners
- Third-party service providers

Typical contractual obligations include:

- Information security requirements
- Data protection clauses
- Incident notification requirements
- Service availability commitments
- Audit rights
- Confidentiality obligations
